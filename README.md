## 📱 About This App

This is the official **mobile-friendly online ordering app** for
**Thirupathi Ice Cream**, Madhavaram, Chennai.

Customers can browse our full ice cream menu, add items to cart,
enter delivery details, and place orders directly via WhatsApp.
Payment is **Cash on Delivery** — no online payment required.

## ✨ Features

- 🍦 **28+ Ice Cream Products** across 4 categories
- 🔍 **Search & Filter** by category
- 🛒 **Add to Cart** with quantity controls

## 📲 How to Install as App on Phone

**Android (Chrome):**
1. Open https://order.thirupathiicecream.in in Chrome
2. Tap the 3-dot menu → "Add to Home Screen"
3. App icon appears on your home screen

**iPhone (Safari):**
1. Open https://order.thirupathiicecream.in in Safari
2. Tap the Share button → "Add to Home Screen"
3. App icon appears on your home screen
